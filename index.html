Refuge at

"After the last run, the fire is already lit."

The Powder Peak Lodge has welcomed skiers for over six decades. Stone walls, timber beams, and a kitchen that takes alpine cuisine seriously. Sixty rooms, two restaurants, and a spa with views of the glacier.

Open from first lift to last light — and well beyond.
